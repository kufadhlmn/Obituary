Tsa Bophelo ba Mamokete Matumelo Pauline Mofokeng

Mofu o hlahile ka la 24 June 1925
❀ ❀ ❀

Mamokete Matumelo Pauline Mofokeng e ne e le ngwana wa Mme Mamosondo Motaung le Ntate Sedikane Motaung ba seng ba mo etelletse pele. O hlahetse seterekeng sa Arlington.

E ne e le yena wa ho qetela lelapeng la Motaung, hape e le yena wa ho qetela ngwetsi ya Phokeng.

O ile a kena lenyalong le ntate Stulu Kleinbooi Mofokeng a seng mo etelletse pele, mme ba hlohonolofatswa ka bana ba 12, ba 6 ba se ba mo etelletse pele, ho setse ba 6.

Mofu o ile a hlohonolofatswa hape ka ditloholo tse 14 le ditloholwana tse 14.

O ile a kula nako e telele. A kena sepetlele ka la 8 November 2025, mme a tswa ka la 18 November 2025. Lefu le mo kgaoleditse ka la 24 December 2025.
